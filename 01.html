HOW TO ADOPT

Before Adopting Dogs

We are committed to finding the perfect home for our animals and the perfect animal for you. Below is a little more information about how our adoption process works.

  • Take the time to read about the animals we have available for adoption.
  • The next step would be to register your interest by completing a Perfect Match form.
  • If we have a suitable match for you, one of our rehoming team will be in touch to chat through a potential new family member. Please note, due to the volume of applications, we cannot respond to every application.
  • We would then complete a Home Visit.
  • The next stage is a bit like a first date – where you’ll meet your potential match, learn a little more about them and see if it’s love at first sight.
  • If everything goes well, you might like a second date.
  • You'll come in for a final chat, we'll go through the paperwork and process the adoption fee, and then you can take your new family member home!
If you cannot see your perfect match at this time, you can still register your interest by completing a general enquiry Perfect Match Form.

Adoption Package

Our adoption package ensures that you and your new family member have everything you need to welcome them into your home.
Our adoption package for cats and dogs is as follows and includes

  • Adoption fee
  • Microchip
  • Full vaccinations
  • Flea / worming treatment
  • £25 To spend at our shop for animal essentials
  • 1 Weeks supply of food
